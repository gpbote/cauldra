# Cauldra Game Design Document (GDD)

# Vision

Build a cozy alchemy workshop simulator where players gather
ingredients, brew potions, serve customers, and grow a legendary
workshop.

## Core Gameplay Loop

Collect Ingredients ↓ Prepare Ingredients ↓ Brew Potions ↓ Serve
Customers ↓ Earn Gold + Reputation + Happiness ↓ Upgrade Workshop ↓
Start a New Day

## Core Resources

### Gold

-   Buy upgrades
-   Hire apprentices
-   Unlock stations

### Reputation

-   Unlock recipes
-   Unlock customer tiers
-   Unlock workshop levels

### Happiness

-   Influences tips
-   Improves reputation gains
-   Increases repeat customers

### Energy

-   Limits daily actions
-   Restores each new day

## Daily Flow

Morning - Shelves restock - Customers begin arriving

Afternoon - Peak customer traffic - VIPs may appear

Evening - Last orders - End-of-day summary

## Workshop Progression

Level 1 - Small Hut Level 2 - Village Shop Level 3 - Busy Apothecary
Level 4 - Master Laboratory Level 5 - Royal Alchemy Hall

## Success Metrics

Players should always have: - One immediate goal (serve the next
customer) - One medium-term goal (buy the next upgrade) - One long-term
goal (grow the workshop)

## Design Principles

-   Easy to learn
-   Difficult to master
-   Cozy, not stressful
-   Reward planning over grinding
-   Every upgrade should feel meaningful
